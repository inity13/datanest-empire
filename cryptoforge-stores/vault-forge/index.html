FAQ

What is the ERC-4626 standard?

ERC-4626 is the tokenized vault standard for yield-bearing vaults on Ethereum. It defines a standard API for deposit, withdraw, and share accounting, making vaults composable across DeFi protocols.

What chains are supported?

All contracts deploy to Ethereum, Arbitrum, Base, and Optimism. Chain configs are included out of the box. Same contracts, same security — lower gas on L2s.

Are the contracts audited?

Every contract includes comprehensive Foundry test suites (unit, fuzz, and invariant tests) and detailed security notes documenting trust assumptions. We recommend a professional audit before mainnet deployment with significant TVL.
