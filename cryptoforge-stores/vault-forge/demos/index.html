Common Questions
Everything you need to know before deploying
What chains are supported?▼
All products support Ethereum mainnet, Arbitrum, Optimism, Base, and Polygon. Many also include configs for BSC, Avalanche, and zkSync Era.
Are the contracts audited?▼
Every contract passes our internal 4-stage audit pipeline: automated analysis, manual review, invariant testing, and economic simulation. Audit reports are included with each purchase.
Can I pay with crypto?▼
Yes. We accept BTC, ETH, and USDC via our integrated crypto checkout. Crypto payments receive a 20% discount automatically.
What's included in each product?▼
Each product includes production Solidity contracts, Foundry test suites, deployment scripts, gas benchmarks, integration examples, and a comprehensive README with security notes.
Do you offer support after purchase?▼
All purchases include 90 days of integration support via our Discord and email channels. Enterprise support with SLA guarantees is available separately.
