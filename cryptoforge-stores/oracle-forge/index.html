FAQ

Is this compatible with Chainlink?

Yes — the Oracle Testing Harness mocks Chainlink price feeds for local testing, the Oracle Aggregator can consume Chainlink feeds alongside other sources, and the VRF Randomness Kit is Chainlink VRF V2.5 compatible.

What data types are supported?

The toolkit covers price feeds, VRF randomness, custom API-to-chain data feeds, and DeFi risk ratings. The Custom Data Feed product lets you bring any off-chain data on-chain with update scheduling.

What chains are supported?

All contracts deploy to Ethereum, Arbitrum, Base, and Optimism. Chain configs are included. The oracle node and monitoring tools work with any EVM-compatible chain.

Are the contracts audited?

Every contract includes comprehensive Foundry test suites (unit, fuzz, and invariant tests) and detailed security notes. We recommend a professional audit before mainnet deployment with real data consumers.

What license are the products under?

All products are MIT licensed. You can use, modify, and distribute them in commercial projects without restriction.

What support is included?

The Pro Bundle includes 30 days of priority support. Individual product purchases are supported through our community channels. All products include documentation and examples.
