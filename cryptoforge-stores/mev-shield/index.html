FAQ

What types of MEV does this detect?

The Sandwich Detector and MEV Monitoring Agent identify sandwich attacks, front-running, back-running, and JIT (just-in-time) liquidity extraction. Real-time mempool scanning catches threats before they land on-chain.

Does it work with Flashbots?

Yes — the MEV-Share SDK integrates directly with Flashbots MEV-Share for orderflow auctions, and the Private Tx Relay submits transactions via Flashbots Protect with fallback strategies.

What chains are supported?

MEV protection tools are primarily designed for Ethereum mainnet where MEV is most relevant. Some tools (Searcher Starter Kit, EIP-712 Signing Library) work on any EVM chain including L2s.

Are the contracts audited?

Every contract includes comprehensive Foundry test suites (unit, fuzz, and invariant tests) and detailed security notes. We recommend a professional audit before mainnet deployment.

What license are the products under?
